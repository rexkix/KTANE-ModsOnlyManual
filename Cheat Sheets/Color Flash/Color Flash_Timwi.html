On the Subject of Color Flash

It’s easy to identify colors. Red, Blue, Green, etc. Turns out it’s a bit harder when you display a word color in a different color though...

  • A color flash module will repeatedly flash a sequence of 8 different words representing colors in different colors.
  • The possible colors are Red, Yellow, Green, Blue, Magenta and White.
  • There is also a Yes button and a No button on the module.
  • Only one of the Yes and No buttons need to be pressed to disarm the module, but must be pressed at the correct time according to the rules below.
  • The color of the last word in the sequence determines which set of rules to follow below.
  • Follow the rules down from the top-most rule, down to the bottom-most rule for the block that applies to your module.
The color of the last word is Red:
If Green is the word at least three times, press Yes on the third time Green is either the word or the color.
Otherwise, if Blue is the color exactly once, press No when the word Magenta is shown.
Otherwise, press Yes the last time White is either the word or the color.
The color of the last word is Yellow:
If the word Blue is shown in Green color, press Yes on the first time Green is the color.
Otherwise, if the word White is shown in either White or Red color, press Yes on the second time where the color does not match the word.
Otherwise, count the number of times Magenta is either the word or the color, and press No on the entry in the total’s position (e.g. a total of 4 means the fourth entry).

The color of the last word is Green:
If a word occurs consecutively with different colors, press No on the fifth entry.
If Magenta is the word as least three times, press No on the first time Yellow is either the word or the color.
Otherwise, press Yes when the color matches the word.
The color of the last word is Blue:
If the color does not match the word three times or more, press Yes on the first time where the color does not match the word.
If the word Red is shown in Yellow color, or the word Yellow is shown in White color, press No when the word White is shown in Red color.
Otherwise, press Yes the last time Green is either the word or the color.
The color of the last word is Magenta:
If a color occurs consecutively with different words, press Yes on the third entry.
If the number of times the word Yellow appears is greater than the number of times that the color is Blue, press No the last time the word is Yellow.
Otherwise, press No on the first time where the color matches the word of the seventh entry.
The color of the last word is White:
If the color of the third entry matches the fourth or fifth word, press No the first time that Blue is the word or the color.
If the word Yellow is shown in Red color, press Yes on the last time Blue is the color.
Otherwise, press No.
